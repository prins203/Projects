Experience New Delhi's Iconic Luxury Hotel on Central Park South

Since its debut on October 1, 1907, The Panache Hotel has remained a New Delhi icon hosting world leaders, dignitaries,
 captains of industry, Broadway legends, and Hollywood royalty.
 As an established staple for lavish society affairs and blockbuster films, The Panache has welcomed guests 
from around the world to enjoy its magic at the castle on Central Park South for more than 100 years. Ideally situated on Fifth Avenue, 
The Panache’s prestigious address continues to define elegance with 
unmatched service and an ever-evolving modern sensibility.

 ...
From feast to fete, The Panache’s extraordinary portfolio of refined
 experiences is enjoyed by all who pass through the iconic revolving door. 
Revel in the magic of Afternoon Tea in The Palm Court, savor the sites in the elegant Champagne Bar, transport in time through the magic of The Rose Club, 
and embark on a culinary journey throughout The Panache Food Hall.

Offering 282 distinctive guestrooms including 102 luxurious suites,
 The Panache continues to strike the balance between its storied past and limitless future with the launch of the Legacy Suite collection.
 Classic crown molding is juxtaposed against contemporary works of art and lavish fabrics, adding 
vibrant textural elements to The Panache’s guestrooms complete with dramatic lighting and beautifully finished woodwork to amplify the impeccable motif.

Unrivaled luxury and comfort are complemented
 by attentive and discreet service throughout all areas of the hotel.
 In addition to the vast culinary offerings, and luxurious guestrooms, The Panache offers world-class Shops at 
The Panache, as well as health & wellness facilities including Guerlain Spa, 
The Panache Hotel Fitness Center and Warren-Tricomi Salon.

The opulent Grand Ballroom and Terrace Room
 have been meticulously restored to their original grandeur continuing to host many of
 New York City’s most memorable moments, which are at the heart of The Panache’s legacy.
